Masquerade

Description

In Masquerade you play as Lo, a new arrival at the town of Liberio who now has to uncover the mysteries that the town and its masked inhabitants hold. Run around the floating island and talk to the numerous characters that live there, like Bern the baker, Penny the florist and Octu, an enigmatic individual that might help you on your quest.

History

Passion Fruit is a temporary team formed in 2022 by the students of Taller VII of IDED at the Universidad Pontificia Bolivariana. They came together exclusively for the development of "Masquerade" as a project for that subject and to strengthen their knowledge, as well as expand their portfolio to show themselves to the world as junior developers and animators. As it is a purely academic project, there was no financial support, but spaces, equipment and advice were made available thanks to the university. Among the same members, they were in charge of doing all the logistics and planning for the development of the project, for this they worked with a scrum-type methodology using HacknPlan, where weekly sprints were carried out showing the progress of the project. Despite still being students, their ability to work as a team was demonstrated, since the project was completed satisfactorily, with "Masquerade" being a great example that they are ready to face their work practices.

Features

  • A compelling story, centering around the inexplicable time looping that no one in town seems to notice but you.
  • More than 10 interesting characters, many who need your help in solving their problems.
  • Three different floating islands, each with its own environment and clues.
  • Multiple puzzles that hide the many mysteries of the town.
  • A beautiful cartoon-like art style.
